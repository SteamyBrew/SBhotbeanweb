Web Developers

A web developer is responsible for designing, building, and maintaining websites and web applications. They use programming languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create interactive and responsive websites that meet client needs. Web developers work closely with designers to ensure that the website functions as intended and is visually appealing.

  • HTML - Understanding the structure of web pages, semantic elements, and proper markup practices
  • CSS3 - Styling web pages, including knowledge of Flexbox, Grid Layout, and responsive design technique (like media qeries)

    • Key Responsibility

  • Assist in the development, testing, and maintenance of the Hot Beans website.
  • Contribute to building out responsive, accessible, and user-friendly front-end interfaces.
  • Collaborate with back-end developers to integrate APIs and manage server-side functionality.
  • Help optimize website performance, security, and scalability.
  • Write clean, maintainable, and well-documented code.
  • Participate in code reviews and agile development cycles.

    • Qualifications / Requirements:

  • Understanding of HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript (vanilla JS or jQuery).

    • Technical Skills:

  • Frontend Frameworks/Libraries: Familiarity with or willingness to learn front-end frameworks such as React, Vue.js, or Angular.
  • Backend Basics: Basic understanding of server-side programming and web technologies like Node.js, Express, PHP, or Python/Django (depending on your back-end stack).

    Cybersecurity Analyst

    As a Cybersecurity Analyst, you will be responsible for monitoring and defending our digital infrastructure from cyber threats. You will analyze security incidents, perform risk assessments, and respond to breaches.

    Key Responsibility

  • Assist in securing the Hot Beans website and web applications from various types of cyber threats, including malware, hacking, and data breaches.
  • Conduct vulnerability assessments, penetration testing, and other security audits.
  • Monitor security alerts, logs, and reports to detect potential threats and provide recommendations for mitigating them.
  • Collaborate with the development team to implement security measures during the development lifecycle (DevSecOps).

    • Qualification / Requirement

  • Bachelor degree in Cybersecurity, Information Technology, or related field
  • Experience with intrusion detection systems (IDS), firewalls, and other security tools
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • 2+ years of experience in cybersecurity (entry-level candidates with strong academic background will be considered)

    • Ethical Hacking

    Penetration Tester

    As a Penetration Tester, you will use your hacking skills for good, testing the security of systems and identifying vulnerabilities before malicious hackers can exploit them.

    Qualifications / Requirements

  • Bachelor degree in Cybersecurity, Computer Science, or related field
  • Experience with ethical hacking tools (e.g., Kali Linux, Metasploit, Burp Suite)
  • Knowledge of scripting languages (e.g., Python, Bash, PowerShell)
  • 1-3 years of experience in penetration testing or ethical hacking

    • Responsibility

  • Conduct vulnerability assessments and penetration testing on internal systems
  • Develop and execute complex attack strategies to assess system weaknesses
  • Provide clear and actionable reports with remediation steps for identified vulnerabilities
